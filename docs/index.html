Gansu dance

Gansu has given birth to a variety of folk arts, among which the dance is unique charm, highlighting the strong local characteristics.

Gansu dance not only inherits the multi-culture of the ancient Silk Road, but also continuously innovates, perfectly integrates national dance with modern dance, and presents a unique artistic style. Gansu dance, with its unique regional characteristics, exquisite dance skills and rich artistic value, has become a wonderful work in Chinese dance art. Let’s feel the charm of Gansu dance and appreciate the colorful folk art in this land.
